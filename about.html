About Team Phlipot Racing

Weasel Phlipot • #52

Weasel Phlipot and the Garke Racing team have been competing in UMP Modified dirt track racing for years. Known for aggressive driving and a never-quit attitude, the team continues to chase checkered flags across the Midwest.

From local tracks to bigger events, Team Phlipot Racing brings excitement, speed, and passion every time the green flag drops.
